### Professional indemnity

Professional liability cover applicable to the current New Zealand practice is maintained as required by the *Accident Compensation Act 2001* framework and through medical indemnity arrangements with a recognised medical defence organisation. Cover applicable to clinical practice in Poland will be maintained in accordance with Article 18 of the *Ustawa z dnia 15 kwietnia 2011 r. o działalności leczniczej* and the implementing regulation on compulsory liability insurance (*ubezpieczenie odpowiedzialności cywilnej*) for entities carrying on medical activity.

### Nature of website content

This website provides information about Dr Gładysz, his training, his clinical interests and his published work. It does not constitute medical advice, does not establish a doctor–patient relationship, and does not advertise medicinal products within the meaning of section 58(1) of the *Medicines Act 1981* (New Zealand) or the Polish *Prawo farmaceutyczne*. References to medical devices comply with the disclosure requirements of the *Ustawa z dnia 7 kwietnia 2022 r. o wyrobach medycznych* and the implementing regulation on advertising of medical devices in force since 1 January 2023.

Information about services *(informacja o oferowanych usługach)* presented on this site is provided in accordance with Article 71 of the KEL and within the limits set by Article 14 of the *Ustawa o działalności leczniczej*, which preserves the prohibition on advertising of medical activity in Poland.
